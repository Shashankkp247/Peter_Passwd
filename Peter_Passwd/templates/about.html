*GOALS of this application :

~ To make a centralised hub which acts like a passwd manager and saves the site/app name, username & password in a database,

~ And can be only accessed, read, deleted & added by you.

~ And you don't have to remember all the passwords and usernames and site or app which they belong to,

~ That is done by "Peter Passwd",

~ You just need one password to access them all.

~ In simple words :- "A safe locker for your credentials!"
